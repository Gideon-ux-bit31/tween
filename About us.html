Why Toddlers & Tweens

Experienced Child care

At Toddlers & Tweens' Haven Nursery and Primary school we have a team of dedicated, experienced and well trained staff who take care of our childern.

In addition, we celebrate the importance of parental involvement and we ensure that you and your child always feel valued and involved in every stage of the child's learning, development and growth.

Toddlers and Tweens Nursery and primary school has a full and varied customised curriculum which is fun filled for our Early learners and we follow a standard local curriculum for our primar school learners.

Your Consideration

Our school's methods engage the child completely and make learning a fun activity for them and a personal challenge rather than a job. Childern who experience self-directed learning from an early age, train their minds to see life as a learning experience and regularly pursue learning.

Activities outside of school. We provide a wide range of activities to get the best out of the child both in class and out door and promote independence;
  • Age-appropriate educational toys
  • Reading/storytelling boks
  • swimming classes
  • Monthly progress report book + free consultation during report collection
  • Ballet classes
  • Educational TV content
  • Gardening
  • Piano Music classes
  • painting

A Message From The Headteacher

Teddy Headmistress

Dear Parents,

Toddlers & Tweens Haven school is a nursery and primary school with an extraordinary charm and ethos, which holds the learners at its heart. We are lucky to have a highly skilled and committed team who strive for the holistic development of a child. This is why our guiding principles are customer care, professionalism, collaboration, innovativeness, and teamwork. Toddlers & Tweens Haven school is undoubtedly a unique school with a great environment and a varied, fully customized curriculum filled with fun activities for both our pre-primary and primary learners. We follow a standard local curriculum for our primary section.

Re-Admission Of Ex-learners

Former learners of Toddlers & T weens who voluntarily left may be considered for readmission, provided they fulfill the current admissions criteria. Applications for readmission must follow the standard admissions process.

The Admissions Officer will assess each application to determine if the former learner meets the entry requirements for their desired grade level at the time of reapplication.

Generally, readmission will occur at the same grade level the learner previously attended at our school.

Readmission to a higher grade level may be considered based on the learner's demonstrated academic progress and abilities since leaving Toddlers & Tweens.

Learners who were expelled from school are not eligible for readmission.

Former learners who pursued studies elsewhere may be considered for readmission to a higher grade level upon providing evidence of appropriate academic progress.

All successfully readmitted learners are required to pay all applicable fees, including the admission fee.

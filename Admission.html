Admission Procedure

Toddlers & Tweens' Haven School reserves the right to set appropriate admission criteria for all applicants. Parents or guardians seeking admission for their children must complete the application form (download form) present their child for an interview, and sign an agreement to abide by the school's rules and regulations regarding fees payment, academic promotion, and withdrawal. Students enrolled at Toddlers & Tweens' Haven School are expected to maintain a satisfactory academic standard throughout their studies. In certain cases, the school may offer scholarships to students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance. Parents or guardians who wish to withdraw their child from the school must provide sufficient notice and settle all outstanding fees.
